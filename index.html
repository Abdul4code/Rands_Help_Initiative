The Rands Help Initiative
Rands helps initiatives is a Nigeria based nongovernmental organization that focuses on empowering bright and gifted adolescents (10 -19 years of age) who are disadvantaged by virtue of lack of resources but are willing to learn a vocation and become responsible adults.
Our Mission

To empower adolescents who are willing to learn a vocation to improve their standard of living and that of their family.

To train, establish, and nurture hidden talent to become professionals for the good of themselves and the society at large

Our Vision
To raise diligent, adolescents who in turn liberates their families and society.
Our Strategy
  • D- DONATE items not in use to TRHI
  • S- SELL at give away price to generate revenue
  • T- TAKE an adolescent off the street by training
  • E- EMPOWER for self sustainability
  • N- NUTURE the beneficiary
